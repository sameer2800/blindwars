Problem 1 : Min and max

Given n numbers , find the maximum , minimum and difference of min and max , max and min. with spaces between the output .

sample input :

5
5 3 2 1 8

sample output :

8 1 -7 7

problem 2 :

Find the mean of given n numbers upto 3 decimals.

Sample input :

5
1 2 3 4 6

Sample output :

3.200

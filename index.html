Problem 1 : Min and Max

Given n numbers , find the maximum , minimum and difference of min and max , max and min. with spaces between the output .

sample input :

5 5 3 2 1 8

sample output :

8 5 -3 3

problem 2 : Mean

are you good at decimals ? Find the mean of given n numbers upto 3 decimals.

Sample input :

5 1 2 3 4 6

Sample output :

3.200
